**Complete 1776 push-ups in July. Earn your freedom.**

## The Challenge

- **1,776 push-ups** over **31 days** in July
- That's roughly **57 push-ups per day**
- Track your progress, compete with friends, represent your state

## Why 1776?

On July 4th, 1776, the Declaration of Independence was adopted. This challenge honors that spirit of perseverance, strength, and the pursuit of something greater than yourself.
